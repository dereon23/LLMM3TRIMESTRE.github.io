Beaches

The City of Malaga as a whole is also a very popular tourist attraction because of its numerous beaches on the Mediterranean Sea and its warm temperatures. The most popular ones are the Beach of La Malagueta, the Beach of La Misericordia and Del Palo Beach. In all of these are many restaurants that serve traditional sea food
